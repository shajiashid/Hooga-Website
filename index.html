Built for the wild

A liquid-cooled motor and structural battery let Hooga take on hard trails the moment the pavement ends — no warm-up, no compromise.

Stable at any speed

A low centre of gravity, a long stable wheelbase, and a lightweight frame keep you planted and confident from the first metre.

Ready after dark

Full-LED adaptive lighting and a silent drivetrain make every late ride feel like the road was built just for you.

Tailored to you

Choose from a curated selection of colourways and ride modes, then fine-tune the throttle map to match exactly how you ride.

Industry First

We've made the seat height so versatile, it defies all expectations. With up to a 900 mm maximum seat height, achieved by spacing it up from the 845 mm configuration using our intuitively designed spacer, you can effortlessly convert from track mode to trail mode in seconds. Allow yourself to take on hard trails or enhance your riding posture, without limiting your confidence to keep your boots on the ground.
