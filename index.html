Say hello to the M1S

Built Different. Proven Everywhere.

In its element

Designed for real riding.

16 kW Peak Power

A compact mid-mount motor delivers 16 kW of peak power — instant electric torque the moment you twist the throttle.

0–60 mph in 5.6 Seconds

Smooth, gearless acceleration pulls from a standstill to 60 mph in just 5.6 seconds, with no shift to break the surge.

112 mi Max Range

A high-density battery stretches every charge up to 112 miles, keeping longer trails and daily commutes well within reach.

240mm KKE Fork Travel

Premium KKE forks soak up ruts and drops with 240mm of plush travel, keeping the front wheel planted over rough ground.

Smart 4.0" TFT Display

A crisp 4.0-inch TFT screen shows speed, range, and ride modes at a glance — clear and readable in bright sun or low light.

DOT Hydraulic Dual-Piston Brakes

DOT-spec hydraulic dual-piston calipers bite hard and fade-free, for confident, repeatable stopping power on every descent.
